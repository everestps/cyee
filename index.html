Computer Science at Everest

Welcome to the computer science website!

The Introduction to Computer Science course at Everest Public High School is taught by Mr. Hesby. One of the Summit Public Schools, Everest is located in Redwood City, and hosts this course and a number of similar courses during two-week sections known as "Expeditions".

The topics we cover include the following:

  1. Weeks 1 & 2:
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • Computer Hardware
  2. Weeks 3 & 4:
    • Javascript
  3. Weeks 5 & 6:
    • Basic Artificial Intelligence
  4. Weeks 7 & 8:
    • Game Development

This introductory course is meant to build students" understanding of computer hardware and software, and how each works. This will supplement their job skills later on in life, in many of the professions they may pursue, as well as allow them to explore computer science as a possible field of work.

I am Christopher Yee, the TA of this class. I have a few years" experience with programming, and plan to major in computer science in college.
